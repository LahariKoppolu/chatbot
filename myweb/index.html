Project Overview

Our project focuses on the implementation of a comprehensive AI-powered system to revolutionize the course selection process within the Graduate Education System at Pace University.

We utilize artificial intelligence and data analysis to address current challenges associated with course selection, offering intelligent recommendations, predictive analytics, and real-time feedback mechanisms.

The deliverables of the project include:

  • User-friendly platform offering personalized course recommendations, comprehensive course exploration tools, and secure account management.
  • Secure and integrated infrastructure for managing student data, training/running AI recommendation algorithms, and connecting with existing student information systems.
  • Specialized software for analyzing student data to identify course selection trends, extract insights, and generate reports on student performance and program effectiveness.
  • Online surveys or questionnaires enabling student feedback on course content, difficulty, instructor effectiveness, and overall learning experience.
  • Comprehensive guides for students and advisors on platform navigation, recommendation access, feedback submission, and system utilization for student support and course selection guidance.
